Welcome to Dr. Philip Keddy's Therapy and Personality Assessment Services

About Dr. Philip Keddy

Experienced Therapist and Assessment Psychologist

I am highly trained and bring over 30 years of experience to helping people from late teens to older adults with a wide variety of concerns, such as depression, anxiety, and addiction. I am interested in therapy as a way to promote personal growth and wholeness.

01.

Individual Therapy with Adults

I provide Jungian-oriented and existential-humanistic psychotherapy, including dream interpretation. I listen to help you better hear yourself and your hopes. I use motivational interviewing to help you find the internal motivation to change. I also use personality testing to help us both understand you more quickly and to provide focus and direction.

02.

Collaborative Therapeutic Assessment

Discussing the results of personality testing is a brief therapeutic intervention in itself, and can provide a roadmap for one's future direction.

03.

Forensic Assessment

I work in the following areas of forensic assessment: Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and Trauma and Stressor-Related Disorders, Landlord/tenant litigation, Preemployment Screening and Fitness for Duty, Employment, Assessment of Testamentary Capacity and Undue Influence.
