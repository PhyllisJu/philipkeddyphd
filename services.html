My Services

01.

Individual Therapy with Adults

I offer Jungian-oriented and existential-humanistic psychotherapy, including dream interpretation. "Jungian-oriented" refers to the influences of Swiss psychologist Carl Gustav Jung and involves a psychodynamic approach. This therapy aims to support you on your journey toward greater wholeness and balance. By listening attentively, I help you connect with your inner self and aspirations, while also utilizing motivational interviewing to uncover your internal motivation for change.

02.

Collaborative / Therapeutic Assessment

Discussing the results of personality testing is a brief therapeutic intervention in itself, and can provide a roadmap for one's future direction. I specialize in administering the Rorschach inkblot test and the Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Inventory, helping individuals uncover deeper self-understanding. We can do a brief assessment with discussion of the results, or a more comprehensive assessment and a written report, depending on your preference.

03.

Forensic Assessment

I can offer forensic assessment for the following purposes:

  • Posttraumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) and Trauma
  • Stressor-Related Disorders
  • Landlord/tenant litigation
  • Preemployment Screening and Fitness for Duty
  • Assessment of Testamentary Capacity and Undue Influence

04.

Consultation for Therapists & Assessment Psychologists

I also offer consultation services tailored for therapists and assessment psychologists. My expertise includes the Rorschach Inkblot Test, utilizing the Rorschach Performance Assessment System (R-PAS), as well as the Minnesota Multiphasic Personality Inventory - 2 (MMPI-2). I can assist with general individual psychotherapy issues and dream interpretation. Additionally, I provide support to therapists working with individuals facing addiction challenges.
